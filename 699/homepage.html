Welcome to the Instructional Training Module using eXplorance Blue Software as a Survey Tool


This training is designed to familiarize the LPAD faculty with:

  • The most used types of survey in LPAD,
  • How to develop different types of surveys using the eXplorance Blue software survey tool,
  • How to generate a result report for a survey using the eXplorance Blue software survey tool.

Learning Objective:

The LPAD Faculty will be able to:

  • Analyze and justify why given samples are quantitative or qualitative questionnaires with 100% accuracy.
  • Recognize the functionality of the tabs in eXplorance Blue platform for building questionnaires with 90% accuracy.
  • Recognize the functionality of all tabs in eXplorance Blue platform for generating a result report with 90% accuracy.
  • Develop a questionnaire with 90% accuracy.
  • Generate a reault report with 90% accuracy.
