Instructional Training Module using eXplorance Blue Software as a Survey Tool



This training is designed to LPAD faculty & training will introduce the following:

  • The most used types of survey in LPAD
  • How to develop these different types of questionnaires using the eXplorance Blue survey tool
  • How generate a report of the collected data using the eXplorance Blue survey tool.

Learning Objective

The LPAD employees will be able to:

  • Analyze and justify why given samples are quantitative or qualitative questionnaires with 100% accuracy.
  • Recognize the functionality of the tabs in eXplorance Blue platform for building questionnaires with 90% accuracy.
  • Recognize the functionality of all tabs in eXplorance Blue platform for generating a result report with 90% accuracy.
  • Develop a questionnaire with 90% accuracy.
  • Generate a quantitative or a qualitative report that has 100% of the data required in the scenario.
